A demat account is an account which holds an investor's Shares, Mutual Funds, EFTs, etc. in an electronic format, and can be accessed anytime!

  1. Beginner's guide on how to open a Demat Account!


  2. First find a Depository Participant who offers a Demat Account. They might be your Bank, a Financial Institution or any Stock Broker. For example - Zerodha, Groww, ICICI Securities etc.


  3. Start with signing up on your Depository Participant's platform.


  4. To proceed, enter your PAN Card Number, Date of Birth and continue.


  5. Next you will have to pay an account opening fee, varying upon whether you want to open just an Equity Account or both Equity and F&O Account.


  6. Next you will have to create (if already does not exist) a Digilocker account and submit all the information & documents mentioned above there too.


  7. After which, you will need to fill an application form and submit some necessary documents & information with it.


  8. Necessary documents which you will need : PAN Card, ID Proof (eg. Aadhar Card), Bank Details, Cancelled Cheque, Latest Bank Statements


  9. Further, you will have to submit your Bank details, such as Account number, IFSC Code, etc.


  10. Then you will be redirected to the 'Webcam IPV' screen, where an OTP would be given. You have to write that OTP on a paper and have to click a photo of yourself holding it.


  11. Continuing further, you will have to upload some more Bank related documents such as Bank proof (eg. Cancelled Cheque) and Income proof (only if you have applied for an F&O account too).


  12. You will then be redirected to an Authentication screen where you can continue with the email verification code option (id you signed in with). You will receive a security code in your mail box, enter and submit it.


  13. Then a declaration form will appear on the screen. Read it carefully, click on the agree button and proceed to eSign.


  14. Upon which an NSDL eSign Service form will open up, enter your Aadhar number, request OTP, enter it and submit.


  15. Your online application is now completed. Wait for some days, when your details get approved you will receive your User ID and a TPIN (to avail CDSL e-DIS facility), which would be important while trading.


  16. You are now ready with your trading account. Add funds in the Demat Account from your Bank Account, and now you can start your investment journey.
